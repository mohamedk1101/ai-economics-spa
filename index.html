The Black Box Crisis

When AI drives the market, we lose the "Why." Deep learning models are mathematically sound but logically opaque—leaving regulators and economists unable to explain sudden, devastating shifts.

In a Flash Crash, algorithms interact in a feedback loop, reacting to each other's trades at a speed that wipes out billions in retirement savings before a human can even reach for the "Off" switch.
